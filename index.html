A Little Bakery with a Big Heart

At Sweet Crumbs Bakery, we believe baking is an art of love. Nestled in the heart of Gujrat, our small family-run bakery has been delighting locals with freshly homemade cakes, cupcakes, cookies, brownies and breads.

Everything we bake is made from scratch each morning using the finest ingredients — no shortcuts, no compromises. Just honest, high-quality treats that taste like home.
